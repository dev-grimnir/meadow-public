Schematic of the west-slope layout — blocks are proportional to planting positions and colored by flower color. Hover or tap a block for the species. A working approximation for figuring out where plants go (source: gardenmap.md).

  • South Band 2 is an interplanted mix — Scarlet Bee Balm, Blazing Star, and a single Purple Bee Balm plant. Blocks show proportions, not exact spots.
  • Hatched area = juglone zone around the black-walnut corner; every species inside it is juglone-tolerant. Dashed outline = butterfly territory (both monarch hosts + the black-swallowtail host).
